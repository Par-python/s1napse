What changed vs. today:

  • Headline strip — position is the largest thing on screen (P4/22, 38px). Strategy banner sits inline next to it instead of stacking. One glance = where you are + what to do next.
  • 3-column grid — left = your pace, middle = race position vs. rivals, right = car state. Replaces the current mixed-stack layout. Each column tells one story.
  • Visualizers, not just numbers — slim sparkline for lap trend, gap-bar for ±3s window, pit-window strip showing where in the race you are. None of them are big charts; they're inline indicators that earn the space they take.
  • Color discipline — violet = self / focus, green = improving, red = degrading, amber = warning. Channel colors don't appear here; this is a strategy view.
  • Density — fits on a single screen at 1640×980, no scroll. Live-tab density bucket as we agreed.
